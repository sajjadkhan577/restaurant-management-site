Carbon Neutral Logistics

By 2025, our entire delivery fleet will be electric. We currently offset 110% of our carbon emissions through verified rewilding projects.

Circular Gastronomy

We look at waste as a design flaw. Here's how we close the loop.

1. On-Site Composting

Kitchen scraps are processed in our aerobic digesters, creating nutrient-dense soil that goes right back to our rooftop garden.

2. Waste-to-Value

Citrus rinds are converted into house-made essential oils for our cleaning products, and surplus fats are transformed into artisanal candles.

3. Community Sharing

Any surplus edible portions are distributed nightly to local food shelters, ensuring that no meal we prepare ever goes unserved.
